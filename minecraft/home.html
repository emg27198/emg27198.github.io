Welcome to Minecraft!

Are you just starting to get into the blocktastic game called minecraft? You want to know how to survive in Minecraft survival mode? Do you want to know what will be readily available to you in game and what you have to mine for? What monsters and creatures you need to face or raise to get the items you need? Well look no longer! Here is a helpful site that will teach you what minecraft has in store for you!

Minecraft Materials & Items

Minecraft is a world where you build homes, take down mob monsters, gain achievements and see how long you can survive in game. Here are the two different types of materials that you will encounter in the game. Click on one to find out what materials are in that category!

Renewable Materials

Renewable items in Minecraft are items that will respawn over time, can be crafted multiple times, and are very commonly dropped by mobs in game.

Non-renewable Materials

Non-renewable items in Minecraft are items that will not respawn over time, cannot be crafted, and are very rarely (or not at all) dropped by mobs in game.

Minecraft Mob Types

Minecraft has monsters that spawn or appear in game for you to advance in the game or for your creations to advance in the game. Here are the three types of mobs you need to look out for when you are adventuring, crafting, and surviving. Click on one to find out about these different mobs!

Good Mobs

Good mobs are good because they do not out right attack you when you get into their range or vacinity. These normally consist of farm animals and villager types.

Neutral Mobs

Neutral means that until you do something to incur their wrath then they will leave you alone. These mobs will not bother you unless you bother them first. Some examples are endermen, bees, and spiders.

Hostile Mobs

Hostile is exactly what it means. These mobs will attack you if you get into their range. They will not leave you alone until you run far enough away from them or die. Be weary of mobs like zombies, skeletons, and creepers.

The Two Ways to Play

Minecraft is a world where you build homes, take down mob monsters, gain achievements and see how long you can survive in game. There are two ways that you can play the game, you can enter god mode and freely create things with infinity supplies or you can survive in a world where mobs are either trying to scare or destroy you. Click on one to find out which of these you would rather play!

Creative Mode

Enter god mode and fly around your world with a pocket of infinite supplies. You do not have to gather supplies in this mode and you can set the mobs to be in peaceful mode or have them in normal survival mode.

Survival Mode

Stick it out against mobs that are hostile toward you if you get to close to them. You have nothing and need to gather what you can to survive until the next day. There is also an even harder way to play this mode that will be explained.

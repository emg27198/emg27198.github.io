Minecraft Server Modes

Minecraft has a few ways that you can play in game. You can play with the ability to fly throughout the land with infinite supply in your pocket and at your disposal or you can try to survive the nights until you have discovered the End of the game. How you play is up to you.

Creative Mode

Creative Mode lets you get into the game with everything you need and more.

Creative Mode basically places you in god mode or a sandbox mode. This is a mode where you can create and build without the risk of dying. Monsters don't come after you and you get to fly around, breath under water, and fall from great heights without taking fall damage. Items that can appear in your inventory include: mob spawn eggs and infinite supplies for armor, food, and materials.

There are a few ways to recognize when you are in creative:

  • You have a materials menu that shows all the items that are accessible in game.
  • You can fly when you jump twice in the air in the spot you are standing.
  • Items from the inventory menu are stackable to 64, but when used they amount does not decrease because you have an infinite supply of them.

Survival Mode

Survival Mode is much different from creative and in some people's opinions, a lot more terrifying.

In Survival Mode, you start with nothing and you are required to fend for your self against monsters and mobs alike. You will need to mine to get stone and precious materials and craft with these materials to build a home base. You can treat the mode like its creative counterpart, you just do not have the infinite supplies at your disposal and you cannot just spawn mobs willy-nilly. Survival mode is a mode where you can test your skill like in any other game.

There are a few ways to recognize when you are in survival:

  • You have a crafting menu instead of supplies menu.
  • You cannot fly when you jump twice in the air in the spot you are standing.
  • You can only stack things to 64 and they are not infinite in their usage

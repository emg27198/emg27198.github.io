Minecraft Server Modes

Minecraft has a few ways that you can play in game. You can play with the ability to fly throughout the land with infinite supply in your pocket and at your disposal or you can try to survive the nights until you have discovered the End of the game. How you play is up to you.

Creative Mode

Creative Mode lets you get into the game with everything you need and more. This mode basically places you in god mode or a sandbox mode. This is a mode where you can create and build without the risk of dying. Monsters don't come after you and you get to fly around, breath under water, and fall from great heights without taking fall damage. Items that can appear in your inventory include: mob spawn eggs and infinite supplies for armor, food, and materials.
